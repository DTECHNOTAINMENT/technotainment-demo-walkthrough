Creators already run businesses. Their infrastructure hasn’t caught up.

Today’s creator stack is split across video, memberships, community, commerce, analytics, support, and payments. Strmit consolidates the paid creator loop without forcing creators into a generic marketplace or crypto-style wallet experience.

01 / FRAGMENTATION

One audience, too many systems

Creators rely on reach platforms, membership tools, Discord, Shopify, course platforms, and streaming tools that don’t share identity, access, or monetization state.

02 / DIRECT MONETIZATION

Content and commerce in the same moment

Paid video, live drops, subscriptions, digital goods, offers, and support are placed directly inside the audience experience instead of being scattered across links.
