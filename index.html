Business review checklist.

This is the go/no-go checklist before coding. If a reviewer cannot answer these, the demo still needs work.

Audience loop

Does the buyer understand what they are buying, why sign-in is required, and why access depends on entitlement?

Failure states

Are pending payment, failed payment, access pending, media processing, and support escalation visible?

Creator readiness

Can creators see exactly what blocks launch, and can admins gate readiness?

Admin repair safety

Can support diagnose paid/no-access without engineering and repair only with reason + audit?

Payout truth

Is payout export clearly distinct from manual external payout and recorded completion?

V1 exclusions

No cart, merch, crypto/token, audience wallet, or self-serve uncontrolled creator marketplace assumptions.
